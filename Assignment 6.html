Blue Whale Game

The Blue Whale Game is also known as "Blue Whale Challenge", is an Internet "game" that is claimed to exist in

several countries. The game allegedly consists of series of tasks assigned to players by administrators during a 50-day period, with the final challenge requiring the player to commit suicide.
